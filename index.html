PROBLEM

SPORT MEMBERS GET TROUBLE ON INFORMATION FLOW AND TEAM UPDATES

As being a sport team player me and many of my friends are not having proper information regarding our team updates, match schedules, player info, venue we are playng at, etc. This has lead to unprepared plans, unsuccessful management and missing updates. Because of this we have been loosing track of the team growth,seriousness and interests, selfsteem has been decreased gradually.

How might we allow our team member to access plannings/updates and have a growing team?

Solution

A PLATFORM THAT ALLOWS EACH MEMBERS TO HAVE ALL UPDATES OF THE TEAM IS THE KEY.

The Platform with key features.

>Connect all members in one platform.

>Club and Team Information.

>Upcoming events and scheduled plans.

Club updates is important.

When players get information about the club and its vision, motivation and self interest arises.

Pushing the team with extra activities.

Some sport related games and shopping features in the platform might help the team growth.

INTO PROBLEM ANALYSIS

COMPETATIVE ANALYSIS

As the app was completely based on the sport team problems that I myself and my friends had faced, a series of analysis was conducted taking the top trending phone application in the market to understand the problem more deeply.

App : Sportengine

Pros

keeps your team on time, up-to-date, and united with schedules, team chat, team roster.

Cons

Lots of Features - Difficult to Use.

App : Teamsnap

Pros

Easy to use. Loved by parents. TeamSnap is team management made simple.

Cons

Customer service is not good.Price is high for the features you get.Still has ads even with costly subscription

App : Engagesports

Pros

Easy to use, it puts the focus on online registration. You can manage things like game schedules, payments, and the team roster.

Cons

Difficult sometimes to make edits

*Easy flow of information to all is important for understanding and achieving team goals.

After analysis of competitors and the features I got knowledge to create an app that's pretty easy to use and where there is good flow of information. In my view "Engagesports" was best among all as it is simple and let users to get what they need. As I got into more research I found the correct needs and requirement for the protoptype.

PERSONAL INTERVIEWS

After multiple interviews and research data. I could conclude the main themes and major insights for the designs.

Major insights

Updates on the community level is more important.

Time based goals need to be achieved through the app.

Team members are motivated through continuous communication and information from the team.

Planning for informed activities lead to more success rates.

Enable each members to access the team updates and scheduled events.
